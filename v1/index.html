Work

    Gemfields

    Gemfields is a supplier of gemstomes. They wanted an ad that offered a general introduction to their brand and product.

    The ad’s opening screen includes a vertical video with the branding and a brief intro overtop. Users scroll to view the next screen, where they can tap on the gemstones to read about and view each.

    Jack Wolfskin

    Jack Wolfskin is an outdoors brand that was exploring geocaching in an app environment.

    The proposal I designed allowed users to search for ‘caches’ on a map and compete with others for prizes.
